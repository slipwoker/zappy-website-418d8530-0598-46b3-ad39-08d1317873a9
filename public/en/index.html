NEWTON Standard

We specialize in merging time-honored tradition with contemporary design. Every suitcase we import is carefully selected with a commitment to quality materials, precise handwork, and attention to the smallest details. We don't just sell luggage – we offer a partnership for the journey, an accessory that accompanies you on every trip and reflects your taste and quiet confidence.

Our supply sources span European workshops and select manufacturers, ensuring that every stitch, every zipper, and every handle stands the test of time. We believe that quality luggage is an investment – not just for the next trip, but for decades of travel.

Designed to look exceptional today – and for many decades to come.

Built as Family Heritage

Every NEWTON suitcase is the product of meticulous craftsmanship and attention to the smallest details. We believe that a quality product is an investment for generations, which is why we choose durable materials, excellent hardware, and finishes that stand the test of time.

Select Material Choice

We select only first-class raw materials: hand-processed Italian leather, fabrics durable for years, and noble metals that never lose their shine.

High-Standard Hardware

Every zipper, lock, and buckle is carefully selected. We use Swiss and Italian hardware that undergoes rigorous durability testing before assembly.

Perfect Interior Precision

The suitcase interior is meticulously designed: double stitching, quality lining, organized compartments, and securing straps that ensure your belongings stay in place.

Finish and Patina

The final finish is done by hand: polishing, buffing, and processing protective layers that give the product a classic appearance that improves with age.
