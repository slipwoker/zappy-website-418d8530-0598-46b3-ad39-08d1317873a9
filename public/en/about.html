A Modern Home for Classic Travel

At NEWTON, we believe that a suitcase is much more than a travel tool – it's an expression of character, style and refined taste. We specialize in importing and marketing classic luxury suitcases, carefully selected from leading fashion houses and brands with rich heritage. Every item in our collection represents a perfect blend of timeless design, uncompromising quality and functional innovation.

Our curation philosophy is simple: we seek suitcases that tell a story, built by hand from select materials, and that radiate quiet elegance. This is luxury in motion – where every journey becomes a refined experience, every detail speaks of attention and care, and every traveler carries with them a piece of heritage. We don't just sell suitcases; we offer entry into a world of sophisticated travel, where style and quality are top priorities.

The Journey Begins

Establishing our original curation approach – meticulous selection of classic suitcases from leading European manufacturers, with emphasis on material quality and long-term durability.

Refining Standards

Developing stringent product selection criteria – thorough quality testing, design and functionality assessment, and establishing direct partnerships with select craftsmen.

Expanding the Collection

Adding additional classic silhouettes – hard suitcases, soft leather bags, and luxury handbags – while maintaining timeless design principles and distinctive elegance.

Commitment to Service

Establishing a long-term service culture – personal customer guidance, professional consultation, and ongoing support to ensure every suitcase serves its owner on many journeys for years to come.

Unchanging Principles

For decades, NEWTON has represented excellence in the world of suitcases. Our values are not just words – they are the foundation for every product we offer and every experience we create.

Integrity in Materials

We choose only the finest materials – select Italian leather, durable fabrics and first-class metal accessories. Every component undergoes rigorous testing to ensure durability and quality for years to come.

Precision in Details

Every stitch, every zipper, every rounded corner – everything is planned and manufactured with utmost precision. We believe that excellence lies in the small details, so we invest maximum attention at every stage of the production process.

Elegance in Restraint

Classic design needs no unnecessary embellishments. Our suitcases radiate luxury and refinement through clean lines, perfect proportions and deep colors. We prefer timeless beauty over fleeting trends.

Service with Discretion

We treat every customer with a personal and respectful level of service. Our team is available for professional consultation, personalized recommendations and ongoing support – always in a gentle and respectful manner.
